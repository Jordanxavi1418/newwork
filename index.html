About

Time is Eternal is an expressive website designed and developed by Jordan Ford. Time is always going on no matter if we accept it or not, when someone is going to bed another person is waking up for work, everything is cyclical! The site is a collection of live streams from cities and news channels across the world making up contrasting time zones.

To interact with the website videos please click and drag the text above the video in order to move them, and if you would like to hide or show any video click the city or news feed on the left hand side of the screen and it will toggle on and off.
